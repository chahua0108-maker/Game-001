# 两个提案的原画方向图提示词

目标不是生成零散原画，也不是生成海报壁纸，而是给每个游戏提案生成 `A/B/C` 三套不同的美术方向板。每张图都应该是一张 `single cohesive game art direction board`，在同一张 16:9 画布里放下角色、玩法瞬间、敌人、卡片/道具、材质和色彩。

## 总原则

每张图都要强调：

```text
single cohesive game art direction board
one canvas
complete visual design set
card-driven game concept
organized art director pitch board
not separate images
not a poster
not a cinematic wallpaper
16:9 landscape
no written labels
no logo
no watermark
```

不要让模型自由理解“红线”或“处刑”。尤其是红线提案里，红线不应该是到处飞的血线、束缚线、切割线，而应该主要是：

```text
red ink strike-through
cancellation stamp
audit seal
punched card holes
redacted case file
card-driven combat
```

需要避免：

```text
blood, gore, exploding flesh, body horror, vampire hunter, tangled red strings,
bondage-like wires, torture devices, seductive framing, overly complex occult symbols,
generic gothic armor, unreadable collage, text labels, logo, watermark
```

## 提案一：红线清算局：心跳处刑

### A 方向：红笔清算局

用途：市场首屏方向。重点是“清算局”和“红笔划掉目标”，不要做成红线猎魔人。

```text
A single cohesive game art direction board for "Redline Clearance Bureau: Heartbeat Verdict", a card-driven roguelite action game.

This direction is about a supernatural audit bureau, red ink cancellation, case files, and precise card verdicts. The redline must be shown as red ink strike-through marks printed on cards and dossiers, not as physical wires, not as blood, not as torture.

The board should contain one complete visual design set on a single 16:9 landscape canvas.

Left side: the main adult playable character, a cold bureau auditor-executor with a clean readable silhouette, tailored black office coat, off-white case-folder lining, muted brass accessories, red ink pen, compact stamp tool, and a small deck of red-marked case cards.

Center: a large gameplay key moment inside a supernatural archive office. The character throws three physical case cards into the air. Each card projects a clean red strike-through mark onto paper-contract enemies, marking them for clearance. Enemies dissolve into paper ash and red ink sparks, not blood.

Right side: two enemy types and one boss silhouette. Enemy 1 is a hunched debt phantom made of stacked receipts and torn contracts. Enemy 2 is a tall sealed debtor with paper talismans and an empty heart-shaped audit seal on the chest. The boss is a massive ledger judge with many case folders as armor.

Bottom strip: card backs, red ink strike-through samples, cancellation stamps, audit seals, punched card holes, redacted case files, material studies of paper, black cloth, brass, red ink, and four tiny gameplay thumbnails showing card play, mark, stamp, and clearance.

Visual focus: card-based combat, red ink cancellation, audit-bureau fantasy, clear attack readability, simple iconic shapes, strong game UI object language.
World tone: supernatural finance bureau, occult paperwork, debt clearance, cold office ritual, stylish but PG-13.
Color palette: charcoal black, off-white paper, muted brass, deep red ink, small pale cyan highlights.
Mood: tense, precise, clever, dangerous, professional.

Composition: organized art director pitch board, clean layout, readable from a small screen, not a poster, not a wallpaper, not separate images.
Style: painterly game concept art, production design quality, strong shape language, clear worldbuilding.

Avoid: blood, gore, exploding flesh, body horror, vampire hunter, tangled red strings, bondage-like wires, torture devices, overly complex occult symbols, generic gothic armor, seductive framing, unreadable collage, text labels, logo, watermark.
```

### B 方向：卡片构筑清算引擎

用途：机制可读方向。重点是让人一眼看出这是卡片构筑游戏，攻击来自卡片组合，而不是角色手里的武器。

```text
A single cohesive game art direction board for "Redline Clearance Bureau: Heartbeat Verdict", focused on the card-combo combat system of a roguelite deckbuilder action game.

This direction must make the cards the hero of the image. The redline is a graphic rule printed on physical cards: red strike-through marks, audit stamps, punched holes, barcode-like redactions, and cancellation frames. Do not show red wires, blood lines, or whip-like threads.

Create one complete design board on a single 16:9 landscape canvas.

Center: a large readable gameplay composition showing five oversized physical cards arranged like a tactical combo chain. The cards have different silhouettes and functions: Mark, Audit, Delay, Interest, Clearance. From the cards, simple red ink symbols project onto enemies as clean game-readable attack shapes: straight strike-through, circular stamp, dotted audit trail, and punched-hole burst.

Left side: the main adult playable character is smaller than in the character-focused direction, shown as a calm bureau operator standing behind the floating cards. Her silhouette is simple, practical, and readable: short dark coat, white shirt, red tie, card holster, stamp device, no giant weapon.

Right side: enemy lineup designed for gameplay clarity. A small swarm enemy made of unpaid slips, a medium shield enemy carrying a locked ledger, and a boss shaped like a walking archive cabinet with a glowing heart-rate receipt window.

Bottom strip: detailed card design system, card backs, suit symbols, upgrade pips, punched card holes, red cancellation variants, stamp shapes, deck tray, discard pile, material swatches, and tiny thumbnails showing card draw, combo link, enemy mark, and screen clear.

Visual focus: deckbuilding, combo readability, physical card objects, red ink rules, modular UI motifs, mobile-screen clarity.
World tone: bureaucratic occult finance, supernatural office tools, ritualized debt clearance.
Color palette: warm paper white, ink black, deep red, dull brass, faded teal-gray for enemy ghosts.
Mood: clever, systematic, sharp, controlled, less gothic and more game-system readable.

Composition: art director system board, one canvas, clear zones, 16:9 landscape, highly readable, no written labels, no logo, no watermark, not a poster, not a wallpaper.
Style: clean painterly production concept art, strong graphic design language, card game art direction sheet.

Avoid: character-dominant poster, tangled red strings, red laser web, blood, gore, vampire hunter armor, torture devices, sexy framing, excessive black-red gothic decoration, unreadable tiny cards, random tarot symbols.
```

### C 方向：心跳判定与清除

用途：玩法情绪方向。重点是“心跳节奏”和“判定窗口”，不要做成血腥处刑。

```text
A single cohesive game art direction board for "Redline Clearance Bureau: Heartbeat Verdict", focused on heartbeat timing, verdict windows, and clean non-gory enemy clearance.

This direction should feel like a tense rhythm of audit decisions. The redline is a heartbeat trace printed across cards and case files, combined with red approval and cancellation marks. It is not physical string, not blood, not a cutting weapon.

Design one complete pitch board on a single 16:9 landscape canvas.

Center: a large gameplay moment showing a heartbeat timing window. Three physical verdict cards hover over a circular audit floor diagram. The enemy group is frozen inside a red heartbeat waveform and cleanly converted into paper fragments, ink mist, and stamped case folders. The visual message should be: play card, hit heartbeat window, mark target, clear target.

Left side: the main adult playable character as a heartbeat verdict officer. Her design is cleaner and more medical-audit than gothic: dark bureau coat, pale gloves, red pulse-line brooch, small card scanner, compact stamp, calm face, readable silhouette.

Right side: two enemies and one boss. Enemy 1 is a nervous debtor ghost with a visible paper heartbeat receipt. Enemy 2 is a ticking interest collector with clock and ledger motifs. Boss is a giant heart-ledger machine, part filing cabinet and part metronome, with a huge stamped verdict window instead of gore.

Bottom strip: heartbeat card variants, timing window UI motifs without text, pulse-line patterns, stamp animations, paper-to-ink clearance effect studies, color palette, and tiny gameplay thumbnails showing early hit, perfect hit, chain verdict, and failed verdict.

Visual focus: rhythm-like card timing, heartbeat marks, verdict clarity, satisfying non-bloody clearance, strong gameplay readability.
World tone: supernatural audit room, medical finance ritual, cold precision, pressure without gore.
Color palette: black ink, ivory paper, medical off-white, deep red pulse line, muted brass, small electric cyan timing accents.
Mood: tense, precise, focused, high-stakes, PG-13.

Composition: organized game concept board, one canvas, 16:9 landscape, clean visual zones, readable from small screen, no written labels, no logo, no watermark.
Style: painterly production concept art with graphic UI-object design, clear silhouettes, not a marketing poster.

Avoid: blood, exposed organs, exploding body parts, vampire hunter fantasy, gothic torture room, red wire webs, whip-like threads, seductive framing, horror splatter, generic tarot collage, text labels.
```

## 提案二：断轨夜车：零号车票

### A 方向：哥特葬礼夜车

用途：市场首屏方向。重点是列车长、夜车走廊、车票变攻击的第一眼幻想。

```text
A single cohesive game art direction board for "Derail Night Train: Ticket Zero", a card-driven roguelite survival action game set on a haunted night train.

This direction is about an elegant funeral night train, a conductor heroine, physical tickets as cards, and undead passengers pressing through narrow carriage doors. The image must show that the tickets are playable cards, not just decoration.

Create one complete visual design set on a single 16:9 landscape canvas.

Left side: the main adult playable character, a sharp female night-train conductor with a black veil cap, fitted conductor coat, brass ticket puncher, ticket-card deck holder, red door-light accent, and a clean readable silhouette.

Center: one large gameplay key moment inside a narrow train corridor. The conductor plays three glowing physical ticket cards. The cards turn into rail-line attacks, seat-row barriers, and punched-hole projectiles that strike undead passengers entering from the far red-lit door.

Right side: two enemy types and one boss silhouette. Enemy 1 is a thin dead commuter holding a torn ticket. Enemy 2 is a heavy luggage porter ghost with chained suitcases. Boss is the Funeral Inspector, a tall veiled ticket examiner with many old tickets hanging from the coat.

Bottom strip: ticket-card designs, punched holes, conductor tools, timetable scraps without readable text, brass materials, black velvet, red door light, cold window glass, color palette, and small gameplay thumbnails showing ticket draw, punch, rail attack, and door breach.

Visual focus: conductor fantasy, haunted train pressure, ticket-as-card combat, narrow-lane readability, strong character silhouette.
World tone: gothic railway, funeral procession, supernatural commute, elegant danger.
Color palette: black velvet, smoke gray, aged ticket paper, brass, red signal light, cold moon blue.
Mood: elegant, haunted, urgent, stylish, PG-13.

Composition: organized art director pitch board, one canvas, 16:9 landscape, complete visual design set, readable from small screen, not separate images, not a poster, not a wallpaper.
Style: painterly game concept art, production design quality, clear worldbuilding, strong shape language.

Avoid: slow mystery-adventure mood, generic steampunk, pure wallpaper, unreadable darkness, random tarot cards, gore, excessive blood, text labels, logo, watermark.
```

### B 方向：车票卡牌杀戮机器

用途：机制可读方向。重点是“车票就是卡牌”，卡片物件化要比角色更重要。

```text
A single cohesive game art direction board for "Derail Night Train: Ticket Zero", focused on the physical ticket-card combat system.

This direction must make the ticket cards the central design language. Every card should feel like a real train ticket object: punched holes, route strips, seat numbers as abstract marks, metal rivets, stamp layers, burned edges, transfer seals, and upgrade sockets. Avoid abstract floating fantasy cards.

Design one complete system board on a single 16:9 landscape canvas.

Center: a large tactical gameplay view of a train-carriage battle lane. Six oversized ticket cards are arranged as a combo engine: Punch, Transfer, Delay, Overbook, Coupler, Zero Ticket. Each ticket card fires a different clear visual effect into the carriage: rail-line beam, punched-hole burst, seat-row trap, luggage barrier, reroute arc, and emergency brake sweep.

Left side: the playable conductor is shown as a functional operator beside the card engine, holding a puncher and feeding tickets into a brass validation machine. Her outfit is practical, iconic, and less ornate than the character-focused version.

Right side: enemy designs built around ticket-rule interactions: fare-evader swarm with torn tickets, armored inspector ghost with locked ticket gates, and a boss shaped like a walking ticket validation machine fused with a train door.

Bottom strip: detailed ticket card backs, punch patterns, combo connector marks, route-line symbols, discard pile, burned ticket resource, brass validator device, material studies, color palette, and tiny thumbnails showing draw, punch, combo, overheat, reroute, and clear.

Visual focus: deckbuilding clarity, physical ticket cards, combo chain readability, railway UI objects, tactile mechanics, game-system presentation.
World tone: haunted railway bureaucracy, mechanical ticket rituals, supernatural transit rules.
Color palette: aged cream tickets, black ink, brass, deep carriage red, green signal accent, cold steel gray.
Mood: clever, mechanical, tense, satisfying, highly readable.

Composition: clean art director system board, one canvas, 16:9 landscape, clear visual zones, card objects large enough to understand, no written labels, no logo, no watermark, not a poster.
Style: production concept art mixed with prop design sheet and gameplay readability board.

Avoid: pretty train scenery without mechanics, character-only poster, abstract magic cards, unreadable tiny UI, generic steampunk gears, slow detective mood, gore, text labels, logo, watermark.
```

### C 方向：断钩清场爆发

用途：爽感爆发方向。重点是构筑爆发后“断开车厢清屏”，体现最强记忆点。

```text
A single cohesive game art direction board for "Derail Night Train: Ticket Zero", focused on the signature screen-clear moment: detaching a haunted carriage with the Zero Ticket.

This direction is about high-impact gameplay payoff. The player builds a ticket-card combo, punches the Zero Ticket, breaks the coupler, and the rear carriage detaches as a supernatural screen-clear attack. Keep the ticket-card system visible, not just a disaster scene.

Create one complete pitch board on a single 16:9 landscape canvas.

Center: a dramatic but readable gameplay moment inside and outside the train at once. The conductor slams a brass puncher into the Zero Ticket card. A glowing punched hole aligns with the train coupler. The rear carriage snaps away, undead passengers and debris pulled backward into ghostly rail light. The effect is powerful, satisfying, and PG-13, with no gore.

Left side: the main playable conductor in a more action-focused silhouette: coat lifted by wind, puncher raised, ticket-card deck strapped at the waist, emergency lantern, clear stance, iconic profile.

Right side: enemy and boss designs related to derailment rules: coupler parasite, emergency-brake specter, and the Carriage Eater boss, a massive ghost train mouth formed from doors, windows, and ticket stubs.

Bottom strip: Zero Ticket card design, coupler mechanism, emergency brake card, broken rail-line motifs, detached carriage thumbnails, screen-clear VFX studies, ticket punch shapes, material studies of brass, steel, glass, old paper, signal red, and tiny gameplay thumbnails showing build-up, punch, coupler break, carriage detach, and clear.

Visual focus: signature gameplay payoff, card combo explosion, train coupler break, screen-clear readability, physical ticket trigger, strong action silhouette.
World tone: supernatural railway disaster, haunted transit rules, violent motion without gore.
Color palette: deep black train exterior, red signal lights, brass puncher, pale ticket paper, blue-white ghost rail light, sparks and cold steam.
Mood: urgent, explosive, satisfying, dangerous, cinematic but still a design board.

Composition: organized art director pitch board, one canvas, 16:9 landscape, complete visual design set, readable from a small screen, not separate images, not a movie poster, not a wallpaper.
Style: painterly game production concept art, strong shape language, clear worldbuilding, gameplay-first composition.

Avoid: slow mystery train adventure, static scenic railway painting, generic steampunk, pure cinematic crash poster, gore, blood, text labels, logo, watermark, unreadable dark smoke.
```

## 推荐生成顺序

先生成这六张：

1. 红线清算局 A：红笔清算局
2. 红线清算局 B：卡片构筑清算引擎
3. 红线清算局 C：心跳判定与清除
4. 断轨夜车 A：哥特葬礼夜车
5. 断轨夜车 B：车票卡牌杀戮机器
6. 断轨夜车 C：断钩清场爆发

如果某个提案三张还是太接近，优先拉开这三个维度：

```text
A = character and market hook
B = card system and gameplay readability
C = signature payoff and enemy/world memory
```

评图时只看四个问题：

1. 三秒内能不能看懂这是卡片驱动的游戏。
2. 主角、敌人、卡片物件是否都有可记忆的轮廓。
3. 图里有没有展示“卡片触发玩法”，而不是只把卡片当装饰。
4. 它像不像提案用设计板，而不是海报、壁纸、插画拼贴。
